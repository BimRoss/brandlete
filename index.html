§ 03 Setup How it works

Stand up your first team in an afternoon.

Three steps from CSV roster to your first at-risk report. No deck. No setup fees. No app-store gymnastics.

i.

Import your roster.

CSV or copy-paste. Athletes and parents get a one-tap invite. No app store gymnastics — works in the browser.

ii.

Set goals & weekly work.

Use a template (soccer, baseball, basketball, hockey, lacrosse) or build your own. Assign once, reuse every week.

iii.

Watch the dashboard.

Engagement drops, missed check-ins, and at-risk athletes surface automatically. Step in before churn — not after.
