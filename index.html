Book onboarding ↗
i. Step one

Import your roster.

CSV or copy-paste. Athletes and parents get a one-tap invite. Works in the browser, no app-store gymnastics.

ii. Step two

Set goals and weekly work.

Use a sport template (soccer, baseball, basketball, hockey, lacrosse) or build your own. Assign once, reuse every week.

iii. Step three

Watch the dashboard.

Engagement drops, missed check-ins, and at-risk athletes surface automatically. Step in before churn, not after.
